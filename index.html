Classic Roast Chicken

‚è± Prep: 15 mins

This classic roast chicken is juicy on the inside with crispy skin on the outside. It's flavored with lemon, garlic, and fresh herbs for a simple yet delicious dinner that's perfect for any occasion. The pan drippings make an amazing gravy!

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (4-5 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • 1 small onion, quartered
  • 4 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425¬∞F (220¬∞C). Remove chicken from refrigerator and let sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, minced garlic, 1 teaspoon of lemon zest, 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels, inside and out. Season the cavity generously with salt and pepper.
  4. Stuff the cavity with the lemon halves, onion quarters, and 2 sprigs each of rosemary and thyme.
  5. Gently loosen the skin over the breasts and thighs, and spread the herb butter mixture underneath the skin.
  6. Rub the outside of the chicken with olive oil and season generously with salt and pepper. Tie the legs together with kitchen twine and tuck the wing tips under the body.
  7. Place the chicken breast-side up in a roasting pan. Add the chicken broth to the bottom of the pan and scatter the remaining herb sprigs around the chicken.
  8. Roast for 15 minutes, then reduce the temperature to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C) and continue roasting for about 1 hour to 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until an instant-read thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the thigh reads 165¬∞F (74¬∞C).
  9. Remove from oven and let rest for 15 minutes before carving to allow the juices to redistribute.
  10. Serve with the pan juices spooned over the top.

Nutrition Information

Per serving (based on 6 servings):

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 35g
  • Fat: 22g
  • Carbohydrates: 3g
  • Fiber: 1g
  • Sodium: 420mg

Tips & Notes

  • For extra crispy skin, pat the chicken very dry before roasting and let it air dry in the refrigerator uncovered for a few hours or overnight.
  • If you don't have fresh herbs, substitute with 1 teaspoon each of dried rosemary and thyme.
  • Save the chicken carcass to make homemade chicken stock.
  • The chicken is done when the juices run clear and the internal temperature reaches 165¬∞F (74¬∞C).
