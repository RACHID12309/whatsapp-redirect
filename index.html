The MBN Story

MBN (Moroccan Bloggers Network) was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate food bloggers who shared a common vision: to preserve and promote authentic Moroccan cuisine in an increasingly globalized world.

What started as a small gathering of food enthusiasts in Casablanca has grown into a worldwide network of over 200 bloggers, chefs, and culinary enthusiasts dedicated to sharing the rich flavors and traditions of Moroccan cuisine.

Our mission extends beyond just sharing recipes. We work closely with local communities, chefs, and culinary historians to document endangered traditional recipes, conduct cooking workshops, and organize cultural exchanges that celebrate Moroccan culinary heritage.
